About

What is CivicLens?

CivicLens is a simple tool that helps analyze policies before they are implemented. It looks at possible benefits, risks, and practical challenges so that decisions can be better understood early on.

It does not predict the future, but helps highlight what could go right or wrong.

Capabilities

What it offers

🔬

Pre-implementation Analysis

Understand what a policy might lead to before it is put into action — reducing guesswork and surprises.

⚖️

Separate Benefit & Risk Evaluation

Benefits and risks are analyzed independently by two agents, ensuring a balanced and unbiased view.

📈

Readiness Score

A straightforward score that shows how ready a policy is for implementation at a glance.

🎯

Confidence Score

Shows how reliable the analysis is, so you know how much weight to give each result.

💡

Simple & Explainable Results

Every output is written in plain language. No jargon — just clear reasoning behind each finding.

🧩

Structured Logic

The system follows a consistent process every time, making results easy to compare across different policies.
